Research Farm Aerial Imagery

Stoneville, MS

This website hosts aerial orthomosaic RGB, color-infrared and thermal imagery collected currently and over past a few years. The images were captured by agricultural aircraft at 1,000 – 3,000 ft altitude and UAV drones at 30 – 300 ft for multiple fields in or near the research farms in Stoneville, MS.

To improve website performance, all the demonstrated images are thumbnails that are a fraction of the original size (about 97% smaller). For full size images, please contact us.
